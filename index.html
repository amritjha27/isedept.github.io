HOD's Message
Dr. Shanthi Mahesh

Professor & Head of Department - Information Science & Engineering


“I welcome thestudents into their journey of holding the most important assignment of the day i.e handling the technology in the future. We promise that they will be provided with the best guidance and academic support in order to make them competent in giving a practical shape to their dreams. Every faculty member of the department is fully committed to excellence in teaching. We abreast the students' minds with the latest skills and knowledge. Today’s world is known as a Global Village dominated by knowledge and characterized by frequent technical and non- technical changes. The future leaders of the Industry need to be creative and showcasing profound professional skills. Keeping in view today’s technical proficiency scenario, we groom and prepare the students to face and meet global challenges. I am confident that students will find our campus and the department as one of the best and will also appreciate the friendly environment that we provide to the students. We enable every student to transform himself/herself into a winning personality, capable of taking on the challenges of the world with full confidence.

Wishing you a grand success in Life!”

    Faculty Highlights

  • Received Grants from National-level Council for Technical Education- All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Government of Karnataka- Vision Group on Science and Technology (VGST), Karnataka State Council for Science and Technology (KSCST) and Visveswaraya Technological University (VTU).

  • Most of our Faculty have published in SCI journals and Scopus indexed journals, which are currently the leading indexing databases preferred by almost all universities.

  • To convert knowledge into realizable output, our faculty hold patents concerning innovation and contribution towards Information Technology and Computer Science.

    Student Highlights

  • More than 50% of the students have been placed with two or more job offers in an academic-year.

  • Our alumni's are placed in top MNC companies, pursuing higher education in well-known National and International universities, in some prestigious government organizations, and also in trendbreaking domains like fashion & modelling, etc.

  • To make our students understand themselves, to support & to discover their passion, various technical and non-technical student clubs are initiated and driven by the students.
